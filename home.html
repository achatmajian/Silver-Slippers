Silver Slippers aims to connect older adults with dance instructors for private, in-home dance lessons. Participating in dance has been linked with numerous health benefits - most notably improvements in balance and reduction in fall risk. Sessions are customizable in terms of preferred dance style, frequency, and duration, though at least one 45-min session per week is highly recommended for optimal outcomes.

Silver Slippers LLC started as a research project. An occupational therapist / dance enthusiast collaborated with a classically-trained dancer to develop a multi-genre dance program suited for seniors consisting of two 45-min weekly sessions for six weeks total. Our research yielded positive results in the areas of increasing balance, reducing fall risk, and reducing risk of depression. The results were presented at the 2019 American Occupational Therapy Association Annual Conference.
